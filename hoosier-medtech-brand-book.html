01 — Foundation
The Vibe
This isn't a friendly SaaS product page. This is a company that puts surgical instruments in operating rooms. The brand should feel like the inside of a high-end medical facility — dark, precise, confident. Controlled power. You walk in and immediately understand: these people know exactly what they're doing.

Dark-Forward

Black and deep red dominate. White sections exist for breathing room, but the brand's center of gravity is dark. Textured backgrounds — molecular scatter patterns, flowing red curves — add depth without softness. Nothing feels flat or empty.

Bold, Not Loud

Big numbers. Strong headlines. Pill-shaped buttons in all caps. The typography hits hard — Montserrat at bold and extrabold weights, massive stat counters in Hoosier Red. But it's controlled. Clean lines, generous spacing, no clutter. Confidence, not chaos.

Surgical Precision

Every element has a reason. Borders are exactly 2px. Buttons are always pills. Section dividers are exactly 56px or 64px. Red is always #B4253C. There's no "close enough" — the system is tight and deliberate, like the supply chain it represents.

Movement with Purpose

Content fades up 40px as you scroll. Images slide in from the right. Stats cascade in with staggered delays. Nothing snaps into place — everything arrives with intent, like equipment being delivered to an OR on schedule.

Three Colors, No Compromise

Red, black, white. That's the palette. Hoosier Red is the signature — it shows up on stats, buttons, labels, and accents. Black is the foundation. White is for contrast and breathing room. Everything else is a utility (borders, muted text) — never a feature.

Authority at Scale

50+ partners. 180+ surgeons. 18+ states. 100% committed. The site leads with numbers because the brand earns trust through scale, not promises. The design reflects that — no soft language, no decorative fluff. Data up front, details on request.

"If it looks like it belongs in a hospital boardroom — clean, dark, red accents, zero clutter — you're on the right track. If it looks like a startup landing page, start over."

Section Background Texture

The live site uses a signature background texture on dark sections — scattered molecular / medical-instrument shapes in low-opacity white-gray, with subtle Hoosier Red accent dots, on a solid black base. A semi-transparent black overlay (40 %) mutes the texture so content stays readable.
Section Label Dark Section with Background Texture The molecular scatter pattern adds subtle depth and visual identity to dark sections without competing with content.
Element Value Notes
Background base #0A0A0A (solid black) Section background color
Texture image /images/blackbackground.png Molecular shapes — scattered at random angles, very low opacity (~10-12 % white)
Accent dots Hoosier Red at 25-35 % opacity Small (<3 px) red dots scattered sparsely for brand color accent
Overlay bg-black/40 Semi-transparent black overlay (40 %) mutes the texture for readability
Cover mode background-size: cover Image fills the entire section, centered

Scroll Reveal Animations

Content animates into view as the user scrolls. Elements start invisible and 40px below their final position, then fade up on entering the viewport. Staggered delays create a cascade effect within sections.
Animation CSS Used On
Fade Up (default) opacity: 0 → 1; translateY(40px → 0); 0.6s ease-out Headings, text blocks, cards, buttons — everything
Staggered Delay transition-delay: 0s, 0.2s, 0.4s (increments of 0.2s) Grid items, partner cards, stat numbers
Slide from Right opacity: 0 → 1; translateX(180px → 0) Images, diagrams, video panels (right-column content)
Bounce (loop) animate-bounce (Tailwind keyframes, infinite) Scroll-down chevron arrow in hero section
